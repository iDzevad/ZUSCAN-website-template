About Zuscan

Zuscan is an application that allows users to scan their surroundings for open networks.


Our mission is to provide a seamless experience for users, enabling them to easily identify available networks and connect to them securely.


With advanced scanning technology and user-friendly design, Zuscan simplifies the process of network discovery and connection.
